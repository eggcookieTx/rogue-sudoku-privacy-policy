# Privacy Policy — Rogue Sudoku

**Last updated:** May 4th, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Rogue Sudoku** ("the App"), operated by **Digital Twins LLC** ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, and protects your information. It applies to all users of Rogue Sudoku on **Google Play Store** and **Apple App Store**.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Contact Information

- **Developer/Company:** Digital Twins LLC
- **App Name:** Rogue Sudoku
- **Contact Email:** service@digitaltwinsapps.com

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at the email above.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Rogue Sudoku:

### 2.1 Player Identity

Collected through platform game services:

- Display name / alias (via Google Play Games or Apple Game Center)

We do **not** collect email addresses, passwords, or other personal account information. No account creation or sign-in is required.

### 2.2 Gameplay Data

Collected during normal use of the App:

- Game scores, completion times, and accuracy
- Arcade run history (relics collected, stages completed, items used)
- Achievement progress and badge unlocks
- Leaderboard rankings and seasonal standings

This data is used to provide core functions of the app (game progress, leaderboards, achievements, and statistics).

### 2.3 Device Information

Automatically collected for analytics and advertising:

- Device model, operating system, and app version
- IP address and general region (not exact address)
- Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
- Crash logs and performance data

### 2.4 Virtual Currency Data

Collected when you earn or spend in-app virtual currency:

- Play Points balance and transaction history

We do **not** offer real-money purchases. Play Points are a virtual currency earned through gameplay only. No payment information is collected.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

1. **To provide and operate the App** — Track game progress, display leaderboards, maintain account and preferences
2. **To improve and personalize the App** — Analyze usage patterns, optimize gameplay balance
3. **To show ads** — Via Google AdMob (banner and rewarded ads)
4. **To ensure safety, security, and legal compliance** — Prevent fraud, comply with laws
5. **To communicate with you** — Service notifications, support responses

## 4. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, we process your data based on:

## 6. Ads and Analytics

### 6.1 Advertising

Rogue Sudoku displays ads through Google AdMob:

- **Banner ads** — Displayed at the bottom of certain screens
- **Rewarded ads** — Optional ads that grant in-game benefits (e.g., Second Wind)

You can manage your ad preferences through your device settings or the in-app GDPR consent dialog (EEA/UK users).

### 6.2 Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used. This includes:

- Anonymous usage events (screens visited, features used)
- Crash detection and performance monitoring
- No personally identifiable information is shared with analytics

## 7. Data Retention

- **Game history & progress:** Retained while you use the App
- **Leaderboard data:** Retained for the duration of each season, then archived
- **Logs & analytics:** Retention period varies by provider (typically 60–90 days for crash logs)

You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 11).

## 8. How We Protect Your Data

- All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
- Authentication tokens are securely stored on your device
- Firebase App Check verifies app integrity to prevent unauthorized access
- Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel only

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

Rogue Sudoku is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

## 11. How to Delete Your Data

2. Use the subject line: **"Rogue Sudoku data deletion request"**
3. Include your player display name and platform (Android/iOS)

We will process your request and delete your data within **30 days**.
